About Microsoft Lumia 550

The Microsoft Lumia 550 is a Windows 10 Mobile smartphone that was announced on 6 October 2015. It is the first smartphone to be released by Microsoft Mobile, a subsidiary of Microsoft, after the acquisition of Nokia's Devices and Services division. The Lumia 550 is a low-end smartphone, and is the successor to the Lumia 535. It was released in the United States on 6 November 2015, and in Europe on 9 November 2015.
