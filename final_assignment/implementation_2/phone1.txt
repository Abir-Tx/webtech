Samsung Galaxy S8 is the latest flagship smartphone from Samsung. It is the successor of Samsung Galaxy S7 and S7 Edge. The phone comes with a 5.8-inch Quad HD Super AMOLED display with a resolution of 1440 x 2960 pixels. The phone is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 835 processor and 4GB of RAM. It comes with 64GB of internal storage which can be expanded up to 256GB via a microSD card. The phone runs on Android 7.0 Nougat and is powered by a 3000mAh non-removable battery. The phone sports a 12-megapixel rear camera with LED flash and a 8-megapixel front-facing camera. The phone measures 148.9 x 68.1 x 8 mm and weighs 155 grams.
